Analytics

The Challenge

Today most businesses have a website where they either sell their product or advertise their business. This digital store front may have made it easier to reach people from all over the world, but it’s made it harder to gauge a person’s reaction to your business and to know what brought someone to your digital store in the first place.

Big Business Solution

Luckily, reporting tools came along and made it possible to understand the interactions people had with your website and where visitors of your site came from. However, getting insights from these interactions that could improve your business usually involved calling in experts. First, you had to get someone who knew how to set up the reporting tool correctly. Then you had to get someone who knew what interactions were most important to your business and how to evaluate them.

10Ray's Solution

10Ray is a consulting service that sets up affordable reporting for your business and educates you on how to monitor metrics and pull insights from the dashboards 10Ray creates. 10Ray also provides strategic consulting to businesses who want additional help getting started with creating strategy from insights.
