Who Does 10Ray Help?

For all Services:

  • Small to medium sized businesses

Why?

10Ray is here to help businesses that can’t yet afford a full-time Analyst or Social Media Manager get a head start in the Analytics and Social Media world. Its prices are low and affordable to suit the needs of the businesses it helps.

For Analytics Services:

  • E-commerce businesses or any business with a website critical to sales.

Why?

These are the businesses that gain the most from reporting dashboards. However, if you’re not an E-commerce business but are still interested in getting some guidance on what metrics are most important to the success of your company, feel free to reach out here.
