Analytics

There are lots of great comprehensive reporting tools. However, it helps to know how to set up the tools and what metrics to look for once the tools are setup. With the Analytics Service, you get the setup of the tool and the setup of multiple dashboards.

Dashboards will focus on the metrics that are most important to your business. It’s standard to at least have one high-level dashboard that tracks revenue metrics. There could also be dashboards focused on any of the following:

  • Campaign performance
  • Email performance
  • Product performance
  • Website performance
  • Referral performance
  • And more...

What's Included

  • 1 Metrics Consult
  • Reporting tool setup
  • Dashboards based on the needs and priorities of your business
  • Education on monitoring dashboards and important KPIs
  • 2 follow-up meetings for analytics check-in

Analytics Actionable Strategy

If you already have reporting setup or need help getting started after setup, consider the Analytics Actionable Strategy Service (AAS).

This service provides you with strategy for the upcoming quarter (or next three months) on how to improve your critical metrics. This means, the AAS will...

  • Help you decide which metrics are in the most dire need of help
  • Give you several actionable options for improving those metrics
  • Give you a timeline for success or failure
  • Show you what numbers mean success and what numbers call for further iteration

What's Included

  • 2 Consults
  • 1 AAS Report

Social Media

The Social Media Service is here to combat four major challenges businesses face using Social Media.

First, 10Ray helps your company synthesize its brand and voice. This brand summary is then used to create a Social Media plan for your company. This plan is a guideline for making sure your voice comes through in Social Media posts. This plan tells you when, how much, and on what channels to post content. The plan also includes an outline for relevant and interesting information you can share with your audience each week to help promote your brand.

What's Included

  • 1 Brand Consult
  • 1 Social Media plan
  • Walkthrough
See Pricing

Social Media Takeover

If you want a way to see your Social Media plan in action, the Social Media Takeover Service (SMT) may be for you. The SMT provides you with a month’s worth of Social Media posts across all your Social Media channels. It’s also a great way to give yourself a breather to focus on other business tasks.

What's Included

  • The option to request up to 40 social posts every week
  • One month of Social Media posts across all your Social Media channels

Note: Pricing will vary depending on the number of posts requested per week.
