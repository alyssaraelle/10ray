About the Founder

My 10Ray vision is to bring affordable reporting to early-stage startups and small businesses. As an Analyst, I was brought into startups not only to set up reporting but also to pull continuous insights and provide actionable strategy that would improve the company’s sales.


I realized that not all companies would have a budget for an Analyst. It didn’t seem right that some businesses would have to wait for guidance and insights while others benefited from teams of Analysts. Thus, 10Ray was born. I saw a way to streamline the setup of reporting and found ways to quickly educate businesses on how to self-monitor dashboards for actionable insights.


In past roles I also helped several startups create their voice and brand. In 10Ray I see an opportunity to help guide many more.
