Social Media

The Challenge

Today there are numerous ways to reach your customer but none have become more popular or expected than Social Media. However, there are several questions that Social Media pose that make it a formidable task:

  1. Which Social Media do I use? +

    2. Not every social medium will make sense for every business. For example, if you’re a hardware store that mostly sees married males over 35 purchasing your products, the Pinterest demographic may not be the most worthwhile place to start.

  2. How do I share relevant and interesting information with my audience? +

    3. To keep things interesting you have to give variety to your posts. You want to make sure your messages standout.

  3. How do I create a voice that sticks to my brand and is relatable to customers? +

    4. If you have multiple people posting or no guidelines for posting, it’s easy to lose track of your voice.

  4. When do I post and how much do I post? +

    5. People are constantly being inundated with messages. It’s important to not overdo the number of messages you send and to reach your audience at the right time.

10Ray's Solution

10Ray works to answer all four of these questions by providing you with a comprehensive Social Media plan. This plan guides you through all your Social Media questions and posts. Additionally, 10Ray provides a Social Media Takeover service that can help you get started with a month’s worth of social media posts.
